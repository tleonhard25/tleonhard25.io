About Me

Some fun facts about yours truly!


Hello! My name is Tatum Leonhard, I am 19 years-old and currently a sophomore at Gonzaga University studying Business Adminstration with a concentration in Human Resource Management.

My current plan right now is to graduate in 4 years and go straight into the work force. My dream is to work in Lululemon's HR department, but it would also be cool to begin my career at a start-up company.

A later goal that I have is to get my Masters degree or PhD so that I can teach college courses (teaching at Gonzaga would obviously be my first choice).

---------------------------------------------------------------------

My goal was not always to be in HR though. My first dream was to be either a dancer or a singer (inspired by Hannah Montana) but I took classes in both those areas and decided it wasn't for me.

...you see why that didn't work out

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

More on the personal side of things, my mom is a 7th grade teacher and my dad is a salesman. They are my main inspirations on why I work so hard in my classes; they are the best support systems I could ever ask for. Along with them, I have an older brother and a 4-year old yellow lab.

In my free time I love to read. My favorite book is either As I Lay Dying by William Faulkner or Where the Crawdads Sing by Delia Owens. I also love shopping, spending time with my friends, and watch reality TV/crime shows!
